Audience

Our target audience is business owners in the Utah Valley region who are looking to support and grow their business and also make a positive impact on the community through supporting business.

Personas

Small business owners

These people will have business experience and using our site in order to grow their personal business through networking, sponsorships and being a part of community events. Our site will provide the necessary resources to enable these business to grow and interact with the community in meaningful ways.

Investors

These will be people interested in investing in their community and it's local business. Our site will allow them to get in contact with plenty of local business for investment oppurtunities that will help strengthen our local community.

Scenarios

  • A business owner will be looking to market their services to other business in the community. Our site will allow them to easily connect with the local business
  • An investor is looking to invest into small businesses within their commmunity. Our site allow these people to reach out to a wide range of local business owners
  • A business would like host an event in order to connect with the community and spread awareness of their company. They will be able to do this through our site
  • A local businnes owner is looking for support and is questioning what a chamber of commerce can do for their business. Our site will explain the perks of being a chamber member and help them to sign up
  • A person wants to support their local, small owned business. This site will provide people with ways to help connect and support them.
