Discover Utah Valley

Welcome Back! Last Visited:

Welcome to Utah Valley! Utah Valley is home to beautiful mountians, a strong commmunity, great career and business options, and plenty of options for outdoor exploration!

Utah Valley has plenty of outdoor options for hiking and exploring. These days we all know the importance of time in nature away from the hustle and bustle. Here in Utah there's no shortage of beautiful nature scenes to explore

Art is a vital part to any commmunity. Utah Valley is home to wonderful art and artists, and full of places to enjoy beautiful artwork.

Utah valley is home to a thriving economy, with plenty of small businessess and oppurtinity for growth in the community.

A strong sense of community is the backbone to any place one calls home. Community is important for a societies well being as well as economy

Facts, Demographics, and Statistics

Population

  • Utah Valley's population is over 600,000 people, with Provo being the largest city in the county

Education

  • Utah Valley University and Brigham Young University, The two largest universities in the state of Utah, are located in Utah Valley

Economy

  • The north part of Utah Valley is considered "Silicon Slopes", which includes headquarters for many prominent technology companies
